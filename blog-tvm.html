What Is Time Value of Money? (The Real Deal)

Time Value of Money is a simple truth: Money you have today is worth more than the same amount of money you receive in the future.

Why? Because money in your hand today can work for you. You can use it, invest it, or save it to earn more. But money you get later? You lose out on all the opportunities you could have used it for today.

Think of it like this: If someone offers you a banana today or promises you a banana next week, which would you pick? The banana today, right? You can eat it now, and if you're still hungry next week, you could buy another one. But if you wait, you've lost a whole week of enjoying that bananaâ€”and it might even go bad.

That's exactly how money works.
